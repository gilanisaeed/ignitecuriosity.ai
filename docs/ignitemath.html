IgniteMath: Teacher-Directed AI for Mathematical Reasoning.

We've taken our "Teacher-Directed" architecture and built a tool specifically for the subject where students need reasoning the most: Mathematics.

The Human-in-Control Loop.

Step 1

Define

(The Guardrails)

You set the mission; the AI follows it. Unlike "Black Box" tools that guess what to teach, our architecture allows you to set specific instructional guardrails. You define the scope, the problem sets, and the learning goals, ensuring the AI never drifts from your curriculum.

Step 2

Deploy

(The "Spoiler Block")

Flip the script on passive learning. Our AI acts as a "Spoiler Block," withholding the final answer to force productive struggle. It provides scaffolded hints that demand student output, ensuring they wire the concept rather than just copying the result from an answer engine.

Step 3

Monitor

(Scaling the Expert)

Live insight, not just data. Move from transactional grading to relational mentorship. Our Live Reasoning Dashboard flags "stuck" students in real-time, giving you the visibility to intervene exactly when it matters most, allowing you to "scale your voice" to every student in the room.

Step 4

Refine

(Mastery-Based Progression)

Close the learning loop. You retain full control over pacing. The AI does not advance the class automatically; you unlock the next section based on verified mastery. This ensures that technology serves your timeline, not the other way around.

Define→Deploy→Monitor→Refine→Complete Instructional Loop

Built for Every Classroom.
