We're building AI that answers
to the teacher.

We started Ignite Curiosity Lab because the tools arriving in classrooms worried us. The race to build autonomous AI tutors was producing answer engines — tools that hand students results instead of teaching them to reason.

Math scores sat at a 20-year low, and students were using AI to skip the very struggle that makes learning stick.

Teachers, meanwhile, were being handed tools they couldn't control and didn't trust.

So we built the opposite: AI that takes its direction from the teacher.

We built Ignite Curiosity Lab to bridge the gap between advanced AI and the science of how people actually learn.

Ignite Curiosity Lab builds responsible, teacher-directed AI that amplifies human instruction and elevates student reasoning.

We design from research, keep our systems transparent, and build for the schools that usually get new technology last.

Where learning science directs the engineering.
