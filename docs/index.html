Meet IgniteMath

Our philosophy: human-led, AI-supported.

The most powerful tool in education isn't an algorithm. It's a teacher's intuition.

Most EdTech tries to route around the teacher in the name of speed. We design the opposite: systems that take grading off your plate so your time goes where software can't — working directly with students.

"AI should make it easier to teach — not try to do the teaching itself."

Grounded in research, built responsibly.

Research-driven design

We design from current cognitive- and learning-science research. The goal isn't just a correct answer — it's understanding that lasts.

Transparent by design

Teachers can always see why the system gave a specific prompt. If we can't explain a behavior, we don't ship it.

Privacy first

Student data stays in the school. We designed for FERPA and COPPA from day one, with no persistent storage of student data.
